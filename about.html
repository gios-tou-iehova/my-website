Daily Student Routines

A typical day at Evergreen High is filled with learning, collaboration, and fun. Students engage in a variety of academic classes, participate in group projects, and have opportunities for independent study. Our schedule also includes time for extracurricular activities, clubs, and social events, ensuring a balanced and enriching experience.

Extracurricular Activities

We offer a diverse range of extracurricular activities to cater to every interest. From sports teams and performing arts groups to academic clubs and community service organizations, students have ample opportunities to explore their passions and develop new skills.
